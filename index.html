ABOUT

Formed in 2024, SNAKEPISS is a hardcore band with no interest in compromise. The name fuses the danger of a snake with the raw, aggressive energy of piss - a perfect symbol for music that is loud, untamed, and impossible to clean up.

Driven by hostile riffs and pissed-off lyrics, SNAKEPISS drags nihilism and anarchy back into the heart of hardcore and stands in the way of everything that needs to be hit harder. If you came looking for the unexpected, you found it.
